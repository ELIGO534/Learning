Common Problems & Solutions

Still Withdrawal Pending?

Withdrawal processing time takes a minimum of 5-7 working days.

Ensure that you have provided the correct withdrawal details such as account number and IFSC code.

Check if your bank account is active and not blocked.

Confirm that you have received a transaction reference number.

Monitor your email or SMS for any updates from the bank.

If there is a delay beyond 7 working days, contact customer support.

Verify if your account has any restrictions due to KYC issues.

Ensure that your bank supports incoming transactions from this platform.

Re-attempt the withdrawal if allowed by the system.

If the issue persists, escalate it to higher bank authorities.

Payment status is Success but not Credited?

Kindly check your bank statement twice and wait for a minute.

Ensure your internet connection is stable and retry checking the balance.

Look for any SMS or email notification regarding the transaction.

Sometimes banks process transactions in batches, so wait for some time.

If the transaction is not reflected, contact your bank support team.

Verify if your bank account has any restrictions or holds.

Cross-check with the transaction ID provided in your banking app.

If necessary, report the issue to your payment service provider.

Keep records of all transaction details for dispute resolution.

Visit your respective bank branch for further assistance.

Payment Status is Rejected?

Ensure that you have provided the correct withdrawal details.

Double-check your account number and IFSC code for any errors.

Confirm if your bank account is active and has no restrictions.

Check whether your bank allows transactions from this platform.

If your account is involved in third-party websites, take corrective action.

Follow the steps below to resolve the issue:

  • Click Upload and provide the required details.
  • Fill out the form with valid details.
  • Ensure your documents are clear and legible.
  • Submit your objection letter explaining the issue.
  • Click Submit to finalize the request.
  • After submission, we will forward your objection form to your withdrawal bank.
  • Wait for confirmation from your bank regarding the resolution.
  • Contact support if you do not receive any updates within the given timeframe.

Why was my account flagged for suspicious activity?

Ensure that you are not engaging in fraudulent transactions.

Verify that your bank account is not linked to suspicious third-party services.

Contact customer support for further clarification.

Provide any necessary documents to verify your identity.

Avoid making multiple withdrawal requests in a short period.

Use only registered and verified payment methods.

Monitor your account for any unauthorized transactions.

Request a security check from your bank if needed.

Reset your passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

If flagged incorrectly, submit an appeal to remove the restriction.
