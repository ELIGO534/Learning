Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our internship program

To be eligible for our internship program, you must:
1. Be currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a recognized educational institution
2. Have completed at least one ELiGo course with 85% or higher score
3. Have a complete student profile with projects/skills showcased
4. Meet any additional requirements specified by the hiring company

Yes! We exclusively partner with companies that offer stipends for internships. Stipend amounts vary based on:
- Role and responsibilities (₹5,000 - ₹15,000/month typically)
- Company size and location
- Duration of internship
- Your skills and experience level
All stipend details are clearly mentioned in each internship posting.

Our internships are primarily available in:
Major South Indian Cities: Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Coimbatore, Kochi
Other Indian Cities: Pune, Mumbai, Delhi/NCR (limited openings)
Remote Opportunities: Selected roles allow work from anywhere

About 70% of our opportunities are in South India, with Bangalore having the most openings.

Our streamlined selection process involves:
1. Application: Submit your profile through ELiGo (takes 5 minutes)
2. Screening: Our team verifies your eligibility and course completion
3. Company Review: Partner companies review applications
4. Interviews: Selected candidates interview with companies (1-2 rounds typically)
5. Offer: Successful candidates receive internship offer letters

The entire process usually takes 7-14 days from application to offer.
